1. Information We Collect

At Z3N, we collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Discord Information: When you log in via Discord, we collect your Discord username, ID, and avatar for account identification.

  • Technical Data: Basic technical information such as IP address for security and fraud prevention.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Providing access to purchased gaming tools and software
  • Processing payments and managing orders
  • Maintaining account security and preventing fraud
  • Sending important service updates and notifications

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Limited data retention - we only keep data necessary for service provision
  • No sharing of personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Discord: For user authentication and communication
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential website functionality and user sessions
  • Shopping cart management
  • Google AdSense advertising (where applicable)

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@z3ngaming.com
  • Discord: Join our support server for assistance

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or Discord server.
