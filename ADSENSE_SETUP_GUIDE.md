## Step-by-Step AdSense Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare Your Website
1. **Review all content** for policy compliance
2. **Test website functionality** on all devices
3. **Ensure fast loading speeds** (use Google PageSpeed Insights)
4. **Verify mobile responsiveness** across different screen sizes

### Step 2: Create AdSense Account
1. Visit [Google AdSense](https://www.google.com/adsense)
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Enter your website URL: `https://your-domain.com`
4. Provide your email address
5. Accept the terms and conditions

### Step 3: Website Verification
Google will provide you with an AdSense code snippet. Add this to your website:

```html
<!-- Add this to the <head> section of your index.html -->
<script async src="https://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"></script>
<script>
     (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({
          google_ad_client: "ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX",
          enable_page_level_ads: true
     });
</script>
```

### Step 4: Update ads.txt File
Replace the placeholder in your `ads.txt` file with your actual Publisher ID:

```
google.com, pub-YOUR_ACTUAL_ID_HERE, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0
```

### Step 5: Submit for Review
1. Complete the AdSense application form
2. Submit your website for review
3. Wait for approval (typically 1-2 weeks)

## Ad Placement Strategy

### Homepage Ad Slots
- **Header Banner**: 728x90 or 970x250
- **Content Rectangle**: 336x280 within blog section
- **Sidebar**: 300x250 or 300x600

### Product Pages
- **Above Product Description**: 728x90
- **Within Product Details**: 336x280
- **Below Testimonials**: 728x90

### Blog Pages
- **After First Paragraph**: 336x280
- **Mid-Content**: 728x90
- **End of Article**: 336x280

## Policy Compliance Tips

### Content Policies
1. **Be transparent** about what your tools do
2. **Include disclaimers** about potential risks
3. **Avoid promoting cheating** in competitive environments
4. **Focus on educational value** and user safety
5. **Regularly update content** to stay current

### Technical Policies
1. **Never click your own ads** (this will get you banned)
2. **Don't encourage others** to click ads
3. **Place ads appropriately** without misleading users
4. **Maintain ad-to-content ratio** (more content than ads)
5. **Use responsive ad units** for mobile compatibility
